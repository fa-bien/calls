The only data stored by the app are the ones willingly provided by
the user and necessary to the functioning of the app.
Exhaustive list: the refresh delay in seconds, as set from the app's
only screen, is stored when set by the user, then restored when the
app is restarted.

The app does not communicate outside of your device. The data
necessary so that the app functions, as enumerated above, are not
communicated outside of your device.
